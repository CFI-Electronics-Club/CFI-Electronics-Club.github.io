What We Do?
We're the Electronics Club, CFI at IIT Madras, a group of electronics enthusiasts and hobbyists with a passion for innovation. The Electronics Club was initiated in the year 2013 by 3 enthusiasts with a vision of building a creative space to cater for projects and ideas in the field of Electronics. Today, we are a team of over 30 students from various academic disciplines, united by our passion for technology. We work in a variety of domains such as IoT, Robotics, Digital Design, Signal Processing, and Machine Learning. We also conduct a variety of sessions for the General Student Body. The club is a space for anyone who is curious to experience hands-on electronics, ideate on and curate innovative solutions, explore their interests, and discover new skills. Together, we shall learn, network and grow!

Summer school 2021
Despite the Global Pandemic of COVID-19, we the Electronics Club conducted a set of online sessions as a part of the CFI Summer School 2021, The sessions were split into 4 arenas: Digital Design, IoT, Machine Learning, and PCB designing. In Digital Design Arena, we explored the areas of digital systems, from basic to intermediate level, We also made students design and implement the various digital circuits using logic.ly and Verilog. In IoT Arena, we taught students the basics of microcontroller programming, sensors, and various type of communication protocols along with the use of AWS, to make them familiar with the emerging field of IoT. In ML Arena, we introduced students to ML, taught them how to code up deep neural network models, and built some TinyML models with applications in Electronics. Finally, at PCB Arena, we taught students the most demanding skill for engineers: PCB designing. We taught them how to design a schematic for a circuit and how to convert those schematics to valid designs using Eagle PCB Designing Software. Students participated in all the sessions with great enthu, and interacted with us very well despite the sessions being held online.

Mega Session 2.0
After the huge success of MegaSession 1.0, The Electronics club wanted to conduct it again with even more content and variety. This time with the collaboration of the I-Bot Club, we introduce the Flagship event of our Electronics Club, MegaSession 2.0. With the custom microcontroller board designed by our club, ESP32 DevBoard 2.0. we did lots of projects namely, Gesture controlled Bot, Google Assistant, Line Follower Bot, SmartWatch, Obstacle Avoidance Bot, Self Balancing Bot, and so on., We also taught students some miscellaneous topics like ESPNOW, RTOS, and Parallel Processing. MegaSession 2.0 was a huge hit, Students came in large numbers, bought our Electronics kit, and followed us throughout the entire span of the event.
