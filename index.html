Who are We?

We're the Electronics Club, CFI at IIT Madras, a group of electronics enthusiasts and hobbyists with a passion for innovation. The Electronics Club was initiated in the year 2013 by 3 enthusiasts with a vision of building a creative space to cater for projects and ideas in the field of Electronics. Today, w e are a team of over 30 students from various academic disciplines, united by our passion for technology. We work in a variety of domains such as IoT, Robotics, Digital Design, Signal Processing, and Machine Learning. We also conduct a variety of sessions for the General Student Body. The club is a space for anyone who is curious to experience hands-on electronics, ideate on and curate innovative solutions, explore their interests, and discover new skills. Together, we shall learn, network and grow!

Some of our initiatives

Development Boards

    In-house development boards designed around the ESP32 S3 SoC with WiFi and Bluetooth functionalities, USB and UART ports, and a sleek design.

CFI Summer School

    Online sessions covering Digital Design, IoT, Machine Learning, and PCB designing as part of the CFI Summer School 2021 and 2022, with enthusiastic student participation.

Competitions

    Placed 3rd at the TechnoVIT competion conducted by the Vellore Institute of Technology in September 2022.

Mega Sessions and more...

    The flagship event of the Electronics Club, with over 150 participants in both years of its operation, featuring a diverse range of projects built upon our very own dev board a specially curated kit.
