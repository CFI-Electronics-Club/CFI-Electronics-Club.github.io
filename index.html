We present to you with our very own one-of-a-kind Custom Development Board, designed and tested by Electronics Club! The board is developed around the ESP32 WROOM SoC and comes with WiFi and Bluetooth functionalities. It has an inbuilt IMU sensor used for orientation measurements. It is fairly compact and it has the CFI and Electronics Club logos, giving it a classy look!
