About Us
We're the Electronics Club, CFI at IIT Madras, a group of electronics enthusiasts and hobbyists with a passion for innovation. The Electronics Club was initiated in the year 2013 by 3 enthusiasts with a vision of building a creative space to cater for projects and ideas in the field of Electronics. Today, we are a team of over 30 students from various academic disciplines, united by our passion for technology. We work in a variety of domains such as IoT, Robotics, Digital Design, Signal Processing, and Machine Learning. We also conduct a variety of sessions for the General Student Body. The club is a space for anyone who is curious to experience hands-on electronics, ideate on and curate innovative solutions, explore their interests, and discover new skills. Together, we shall learn, network and grow!
