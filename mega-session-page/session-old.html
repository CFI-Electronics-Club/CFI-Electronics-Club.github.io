We present to you with our very own one-of-a-kind Custom Development Board, designed and tested by Electronics Club! The board is developed around the ESP32 S3 SoC and comes with WiFi and Bluetooth functionalities. It has both USB and UART ports, which does not just allow you to upload your code, but also allows you to connect to your pheripherals easily. It is fairly compact and it has the CFI and Electronics Club logos, giving it a classy look!
